# StreetBites
A mini prototype in which an attempt was made to digitalise the street food sector of the indian markets, by allowing vendors to display their daily location and let users pre order form groups with other users to visit together and encourage communuty building. The vendors can update their daily loctions, menues and users can save time and reach their stalls with ease. Users looking for groups can also form a group chat with people looking to go to the same stall at the same time and meet new people, the group chat will only be accessible through the app ensuring now personal details are shared through censorship and once the meetup is done the chat will be dissolved.
The vendors will also be reminded daily to check the app and be guided through gemini voice assistance and help them update their location through google maps, and a seperate section for the vendors to accept pre orders for people looking to pickup their food in a hurry, and payment would be done in two parts, one for the pre order and the complete in person to the vendor. 
Payment gateway is still in progress for the preorder, the vendor updates would be done through pop up notification and a subscription service for users to see their favorite vendors latest upated location. 
currently used gtk and c to make the prototype but would improve further.
